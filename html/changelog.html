31 August 2015

Zaers updated:

  • The space vines event should now properly spawn the vines
  • Lazarus injectors should now work properly
  • Styptic powder and silver sulfadiazine will no longer heal dead people
  • Stun times for the stunbaton and taser have been increased
  • Skeletons now take extra brute damage and are no longer heatproof

26 August 2015

Koriath updated:

  • Added Alkysine to the Medborg reagents list.
  • Silicons can now interact with the microwave.
  • Iv drips now attempt to remove chems from the body of the patient.

Zaers updated:

  • Aliens now show a proper description when examined

21 August 2015

Zaers updated:

  • Aliens now have internal organs that give them their powers
  • Operating consoles now list the next step of the surgeries being done on the patients
  • Brain removal and appendectomy have been replaced with a generic organ manipulation surgery

19 August 2015

Zaers updated:

  • Walls should now properly get re-smoothed when a neighboring wall gets destroyed
  • Mechs now use an action button based interface instead of using verbs

18 August 2015

Zaers updated:

  • Mint toxin should now properly gib fat people again
  • Security HUDs should now properly update when identities get changed
  • Loyalty implants should now get properly implanted instead of getting silently deleted while using an implanter
  • Space wind should no longer push objects that are being pulled

09 August 2015

Koriath updated:

  • Deep fryer has been added. Runs on corn oil (and regular oil or welder fuel, not recomended thought)
  • Deep fryer gives nutriment, can be upgraded with Science.
  • Deep fryer can add custom chems to food too.
  • Dakimakuras added. Simple customization, will need further improvement.
  • Fixed Toxins Mass Driver.

09 July 2015

Zaers updated:

  • Gangs are now datumized, allowing more than two gangs to exist at once
  • Full blindness while critted is now a server config option, defaulting to off
  • There is now an additional level of blindness that corresponds to the old blindness available

08 July 2015

Zaers updated:

  • Blindness has been restored to its former strength
  • The DNA aide and DNA saboteur symptoms are no more

06 July 2015

Zaers updated:

  • Changelings can no longer activate their regenerative stasis after they are already dead and cannot fully die when they're regenerating
  • Changeling headslugs can now be activated when the user is unconcious (but not dead) and allow the user to track the progress of the egg after infesting

05 July 2015

Zaers updated:

  • The recycler now accumulates the materials from the items it recycles instead of generating sheets from nowhere
  • Cryo now respects temperature resistance on the occupant and displays the current temperature of the patient

Zaers updated:

  • Reverted to the old atmos system

Zaers updated:

  • Change the space transition assignment, infinite space loops should no longer happen

Zaers updated:
